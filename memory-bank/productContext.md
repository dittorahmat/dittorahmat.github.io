# Product Context

## Purpose:
This website serves as a personal portfolio and blog for a data engineer. It will be used to showcase projects, share insights, and establish an online presence.

## Problems Solved:
- Need for a professional online portfolio
- Platform to share data engineering knowledge and experience
- Central hub for online presence

## How it Should Work:
- Portfolio section to display projects with descriptions and links
- Blog section for articles, tutorials, and thought pieces
- About section with personal information and contact details
- Easy navigation and clear information architecture

## User Experience Goals:
- Professional and modern look and feel
- Easy to navigate and find information
- Responsive design for different devices
- Fast loading times
