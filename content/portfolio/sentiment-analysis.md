## Project Overview

This project focuses on building a natural language processing (NLP) model to analyze the sentiment of social media posts related to a specific product or brand. The goal is to understand customer opinions and identify areas for improvement.

## Technologies Used

*   Python
*   NLTK
*   Scikit-learn
*   TensorFlow
*   Keras

## Project Details

*   Collected and preprocessed social media data from various sources, such as Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
*   Built and evaluated several NLP models, including Naive Bayes, Support Vector Machines, and Recurrent Neural Networks.
*   Developed a dashboard to visualize the sentiment trends and identify key topics of discussion.

## Results

The sentiment analysis model achieved an accuracy of 80% in classifying the sentiment of social media posts. This allows the company to gain valuable insights into customer opinions and identify areas for product improvement and marketing optimization.
