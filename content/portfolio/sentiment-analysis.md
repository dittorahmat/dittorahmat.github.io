## Project Overview

In this project, I built a natural language processing (NLP) model to analyze the sentiment of social media posts related to a specific product or brand. The goal was to understand customer opinions and identify areas for improvement.

## Technologies Used

*   Python: My primary language for NLP tasks.
*   NLTK: A popular library for natural language processing in Python.
*   Scikit-learn: For machine learning tasks.
*   TensorFlow: A powerful framework for building neural networks.
*   Keras: A high-level API for building neural networks.

## Project Details

*   I started by collecting and preprocessing social media data from various sources, such as Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
*   Then, I built and evaluated several NLP models, including Naive Bayes, Support Vector Machines, and Recurrent Neural Networks.
*   Finally, I developed a dashboard to visualize the sentiment trends and identify key topics of discussion.

## Results

The sentiment analysis model achieved an accuracy of 80% in classifying the sentiment of social media posts. This allowed the company to gain valuable insights into customer opinions and identify areas for product improvement and marketing optimization. It was fascinating to see how NLP can be used to understand the voice of the customer.
