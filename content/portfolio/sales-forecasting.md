## Project Overview

In this project, I built a time series model to forecast future sales based on historical data. The goal was to provide accurate sales forecasts to help the company make informed decisions about inventory management, production planning, and marketing strategies.

## Technologies Used

*   Python: My go-to language for data analysis and model building.
*   Pandas: For data manipulation and analysis.
*   NumPy: For numerical computing.
*   Statsmodels: For time series analysis.
*   Matplotlib: For creating visualizations.

## Project Details

*   I started by performing time series analysis to identify trends, seasonality, and other patterns in the sales data.
*   Then, I built and evaluated several time series models, including ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing, and Prophet.
*   Finally, I developed a model deployment strategy to integrate the sales forecasting model into the company's enterprise resource planning (ERP) system.

## Results

The sales forecasting model achieved a mean absolute percentage error (MAPE) of 5% in forecasting future sales. This allowed the company to make more accurate sales forecasts, leading to improved inventory management, reduced stockouts, and increased profitability. It was a challenging but rewarding project that showcased the power of time series analysis.
