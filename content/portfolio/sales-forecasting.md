## Project Overview

This project focuses on building a time series model to forecast future sales based on historical data. The goal is to provide accurate sales forecasts to help the company make informed decisions about inventory management, production planning, and marketing strategies.

## Technologies Used

*   Python
*   Pandas
*   NumPy
*   Statsmodels
*   Matplotlib

## Project Details

*   Performed time series analysis to identify trends, seasonality, and other patterns in the sales data.
*   Built and evaluated several time series models, including ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing, and Prophet.
*   Developed a model deployment strategy to integrate the sales forecasting model into the company's enterprise resource planning (ERP) system.

## Results

The sales forecasting model achieved a mean absolute percentage error (MAPE) of 5% in forecasting future sales. This allows the company to make more accurate sales forecasts, leading to improved inventory management, reduced stockouts, and increased profitability.
